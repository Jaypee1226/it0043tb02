AVATAR LIVE ACTION ON NETFLIX

Netflix’s live-action adaptation of “Avatar: The Last Airbender” premieres on Thursday, Feb. 22, and fans of the original animated show have been patiently waiting to see their favorite characters faithfully brought to life after the “Last Airbender” 2010 film.

AIR

Air is the element of freedom. Airbending's strength is its defensive nature. Fighting is a last resort. The essence of this bending form is characterized by a fluid and adaptable nature, drawing inspiration from the ever-shifting winds.

WATER

Water is the element of change. While waterbending can be used in combat, its strength lies in using water's solid, gaseous and liquid states to control and contain. Waterbending embodies qualities of compassion and adaptability, mirroring the flowing nature of this bending discipline.

EARTH

Earth is the element of substance. An Earthbender's greatest tool is the ground under their feet. Earthbenders can strike the ground to cause localized earthquakes or propel slabs of stone through the air. The essence of this bending style lies in resilience and purpose, facing challenges with an unshakeable resolve.

FIRE

Fire is the element of power. Firebending is fierce and direct. Fire is the element of power, and firebending is unique in that its practitioners can generate the element from their own energy. Firebenders approach adversity with confidence and vitality, ready to conquer any obstacle that stands in their path.
